Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

At ZARO Bakerhouse, we are committed to delivering fresh, high-quality baked goods to our customers. Due to the perishable nature of our products and the care involved in preparing each order, we have established the following Refund and Cancellation Policy.

Please read this policy carefully before placing an order. By placing an order with ZARO Bakerhouse, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Order Cancellation

Cancellation Window

Orders may be cancelled only within a limited time frame after order placement and before preparation begins. The cancellation window varies depending on the type of product ordered:

  • Standard Bakery Items: Cancellation is permitted within 30 minutes of order placement
  • Custom Cakes and Special Orders: Cancellation must be requested at least 24 hours before the scheduled delivery or pickup time

After Preparation Begins

Once your order has entered the preparation stage, cancellation is not possible. This is due to the following reasons:

  • Ingredients have been allocated and preparation has commenced
  • Bakery products are freshly prepared specifically for your order
  • Perishable items cannot be restocked or resold

How to Cancel

To request a cancellation within the permitted window, please contact us immediately at:

  • Phone: +91 877 964 9761
  • Email: hello@zarobakery.com

Cancellation requests are subject to verification and approval by ZARO Bakerhouse.

Important: Cancellation requests submitted after the preparation has started will not be accepted. If you fail to collect or receive your order, no refund will be issued.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are applicable only in the following circumstances:

Eligible Refund Cases

  • Incorrect Items Delivered: If you receive products that do not match your order
  • Damaged or Spoiled Products: If products are received in a damaged, stale, or unsatisfactory condition
  • Missing Items: If items from your order are missing upon delivery
  • Quality Issues: If the product does not meet our standard quality criteria, as verified by ZARO Bakerhouse
  • Non-Delivery: If your order was not delivered despite confirmation

Non-Eligible Refund Cases

Refunds will not be provided in the following situations:

  • Change of mind or preference after order placement
  • Incorrect delivery address provided by the customer
  • Customer unavailability at the time of delivery
  • Rejection of order without valid reason at the time of delivery
  • Minor variations in appearance or decoration that do not affect quality or taste
  • Allergic reactions or dietary concerns not communicated at the time of ordering

Please Note: Due to the perishable nature of food items, change-of-mind refunds are not applicable for any bakery products ordered from ZARO Bakerhouse.

3. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund based on the criteria above, please follow these steps:

  • Contact ZARO Bakerhouse within 24 hours of receiving your order
  • Provide your order number, contact details, and photographic evidence (if applicable) of the issue
  • Explain the reason for your refund request clearly

Verification and Approval

All refund requests are subject to verification by ZARO Bakerhouse. We may request additional information or photographic evidence to assess the claim. Refund approval is at the sole discretion of ZARO Bakerhouse based on the validity of the complaint.

Refund Processing Timeline

Once your refund request is approved:

  • The refund will be processed to the original payment method used at the time of purchase
  • Processing time is typically 5 to 7 business days from the date of approval
  • The actual credit to your account may take additional time depending on your bank or payment gateway provider
  • For payments made via UPI, wallets, or net banking, refunds may reflect within 3 to 5 business days
  • For credit/debit card payments, refunds may take 7 to 10 business days depending on your card issuer

Refund Notification

You will receive a confirmation email or SMS once the refund has been initiated. Please retain this confirmation for your records.

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable under all circumstances, except in cases of verified quality or delivery issues confirmed by ZARO Bakerhouse:

  • Customized Cakes: Cakes with personalized designs, messages, or decorations prepared as per customer specifications
  • Baked-to-Order Items: Specialty items prepared specifically for your order, including themed cakes, corporate orders, and event-specific products
  • Perishable Goods: All fresh bakery items including breads, pastries, cookies, and confections
  • Pre-ordered Items: Items ordered in advance for special occasions, events, or celebrations
  • Gift Hampers and Combo Packs: Unless there is a proven defect or quality issue

Exception: If any of the above items are delivered in damaged condition, with incorrect specifications, or do not meet quality standards as verified by ZARO Bakerhouse, a refund or replacement may be issued at our discretion.

5. Late or Missing Refunds

If You Haven't Received Your Refund

If your refund has been approved but you have not received it within the stated processing period, please take the following steps:

  • Check Your Bank Account: Verify your bank statement or payment gateway transaction history
  • Contact Your Bank: There may be a processing delay on the bank's end. Contact your bank or card issuer to inquire about pending refunds
  • Contact Payment Gateway: If you paid via Razorpay, UPI, or other gateways, check with the respective payment service provider
  • Contact ZARO Bakerhouse: If the refund still does not appear after completing the above steps, please reach out to us with your refund reference number

Refund Delays

Refunds may be delayed due to:

  • Bank processing times and internal verification procedures
  • Delays by payment gateway providers or intermediary financial institutions
  • Weekends, public holidays, or non-business days

ZARO Bakerhouse is not responsible for delays caused by third-party payment processors or banking institutions. However, we will make every effort to assist you in tracking your refund.

6. Partial Refunds

In certain situations, partial refunds may be issued instead of full refunds:

  • If only a portion of your order is affected by quality or delivery issues
  • If some items in a combo or hamper are acceptable while others are not
  • If a minor defect is identified that does not significantly affect the product's usability or quality

The amount of the partial refund will be determined by ZARO Bakerhouse based on the nature and extent of the issue.

7. Replacement Policy

In cases where a refund is not feasible or preferred, ZARO Bakerhouse may offer a replacement instead:

  • Replacement products will be of equal or greater value
  • Replacement orders will be processed and delivered at the earliest convenience
  • No additional charges will be levied for replacement deliveries due to errors on our part

Replacement is subject to product availability and approval by ZARO Bakerhouse management.
